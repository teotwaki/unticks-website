CONSULTANT

Helping companies of all sizes match their technical stack to their ambitions and customers' needs is what I do. Are you ready to scale up?

HI, I'M SEBASTIAN

I started programming more than two decades ago, shortly before my age required two digits. In that period, I've had the opportunity to work with various companies, large and small. From re-architecting SaaS platforms used by tens of thousands of people around the world on a daily basis, to protecting a website in the middle of a DDoS attack, and deploying security solutions for banks and governments, I've been lucky to gather experience in a lot of different environments. I've contributed to projects of collossal sizes, and I've helped early stage startups avoid bankruptcy by saving them $50 on their hosting bill.

I'm also a globetrotter, currently based in beautiful Copenhagen, who builds robots and quadcopters from scratch, just for fun.

HOW I CAN HELP

Knowing that your technological stack will scale with your business is a requirement to be successful. I can help identify and fix bottlenecks in your software development processes, in your architecture, or in your code. I can help plan for future growth, and make your platform more robust.

Over the last 5 years, the software industry has changed massively, with containers, orchestration, and automation at the forefront of every company's tech stack. "Devops" is now used to describe almost every single software team, but what does it actually mean? I can train your team, or build the infrastructure you need.

And if you're just looking for someone who can build awesome software quickly, I can do that, too :)
